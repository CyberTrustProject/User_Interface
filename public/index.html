How to install Cyber-Trust SDA on your Android device

  • 1. You can install the Cyber-Trust SDA (APK files) on your Android smartphone or tablet directly from your browser.
  • 2. Click to download the APK file
  • 3. You should then be able to see it downloading on the top bar of your device.
  • 4. Once it's downloaded, open Downloads, tap on the APK file, and tap Yes when prompted.
  • 5. The app will begin installing on your device.
